Apple Juice

How to make it

Ingredients

  • 2 apples
  • 2 tbs lemon juice
  • 6 ice cubes
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tsp honey or sugar

instructions

  1. Wash apples
  2. peal apples
  3. cut the apples and remove the core and seeds
  4. add them to blender jar along with ice cubes and lemon juice, water, and ginger. Add the Honey/sugar.
  5. You don't need to strain this juice. Transfer to a serving glass and serve immediately
